About Us

From the very beginning, we've been dedicated to delivering products of exceptional quality.

Discover Pet Food Kitchen

Our Beginnings
Our journey began with a focus on producing the finest food for human consumption. We take pride in delivering quality products that meet the highest standards.
Our Commitment to Sustainability
We are committed to making our processes as sustainable and respectful as possible. Every step of our production is designed with care, ensuring quality and integrity.
Pet Food Kitchen
Driven by our goal to use every part of the animal and waste nothing, we developed Pet Food Kitchen. This initiative ensures that no part of our production goes unused, benefiting pets and reducing waste.
A Legacy of Quality
Linked to Woodhead Brothers, one of the most recognized names in the British meat industry with a history spanning over 100 years, we continue to uphold a tradition of excellence. As a wholly owned subsidiary of Wm Morrison Supermarkets Limited, we maintain our commitment to quality and sustainability.
Traceability and Control
We are proud to be the only major supermarket that owns its own abattoir and meat processing operations, providing us with unique traceability and control over our supply chain.
Our Scale of Operations
Every week, we process over 3,800 cattle, 11,000 lambs, and 30,000 pigs. Our operations are extensive, ensuring that we meet the needs of our customers while maintaining our commitment to sustainability and quality.
